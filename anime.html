Neon Genesis Evangelion

The best anime I've ever seen. The unique combination of a mecha anime with psychological elements make it stand out from the other mecha shows. Also has a great OST with some use of classical music which is very nice.

10/10 Plot
10/10 Characters
10/10 OST

Steins;Gate

The best plot in an anime that I've seen. Who doesn't like the concept of time travel? This paired with the psycological elements, character development, and an excellent OST featuring mostly piano music makes it a very enjoyable anime to watch.

9.5/10 Plot
9.5/10 Characters
9.5/10 OST

Great Teacher Onizuka

This is the most underrated anime on my top list. I only heard about this anime when I was browsing the band PornoGraffitti's Spotify and came across "Hitori no Yoru", being the 2nd OP to Great Teacher Onizuka. GTO has great characters, each with a unique backstory and each undergoing develeopment throughout the show. The psychological struggles of each student in Class 3-4 and Onizuka's unique ways to address them makes each arc of the show exciting and interesting.

9/10 Plot
10/10 Characters
9/10 OST
